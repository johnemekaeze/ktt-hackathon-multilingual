TITLE: AU Innovation Hub — Cleantech Development Grant 03
ISSUING BODY: AU Innovation Hub
SECTOR: cleantech
BUDGET: USD 200,000
DEADLINE: 2026-07-09
REGION: Central Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Non-governmental organizations with proven track record in the stated sector.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative cleantech solutions that address key development challenges across Central Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
Incomplete applications will be disqualified without further notice. Environmental and social impact assessments must accompany applications. Applications must comply with all regional procurement guidelines. Organizations with prior convictions for fraud are ineligible.
