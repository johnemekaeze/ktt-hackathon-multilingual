TITLE: USAID East Africa — Healthtech Development Grant 08
ISSUING BODY: USAID East Africa
SECTOR: healthtech
BUDGET: USD 1,000,000
DEADLINE: 2027-01-13
REGION: Central Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Community-based organizations with local government endorsement.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative healthtech solutions that address key development challenges across Central Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
The issuing body reserves the right to cancel or modify this tender at any time. A signed declaration of non-conflict-of-interest is mandatory. Priority will be given to women-led and youth-led enterprises. Organizations with prior convictions for fraud are ineligible.
