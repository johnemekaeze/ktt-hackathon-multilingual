TITLE: African Development Fund — Agritech Development Grant 01
ISSUING BODY: African Development Fund
SECTOR: agritech
BUDGET: USD 5,000
DEADLINE: 2026-07-19
REGION: East Africa
ELIGIBILITY: SMEs registered in AU member states with at least 2 years of operation.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative agritech solutions that address key development challenges across East Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
Organizations with prior convictions for fraud are ineligible. Incomplete applications will be disqualified without further notice. Environmental and social impact assessments must accompany applications. The issuing body reserves the right to cancel or modify this tender at any time.
