TITLE: ECOWAS Development Bank — Wastetech Development Grant 24
ISSUING BODY: ECOWAS Development Bank
SECTOR: wastetech
BUDGET: USD 1,000,000
DEADLINE: 2026-10-22
REGION: Southern Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Community-based organizations with local government endorsement.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative wastetech solutions that address key development challenges across Southern Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
Priority will be given to women-led and youth-led enterprises. Incomplete applications will be disqualified without further notice. All submitted documents become the property of the issuing organization. The grant is non-transferable and must be used within the stated jurisdiction.
