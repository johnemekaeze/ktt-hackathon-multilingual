TITLE: GreenAfrica Initiative — Healthtech Development Grant 02
ISSUING BODY: GreenAfrica Initiative
SECTOR: healthtech
BUDGET: USD 50,000
DEADLINE: 2026-07-14
REGION: West Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Community-based organizations with local government endorsement.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative healthtech solutions that address key development challenges across West Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
The issuing body reserves the right to cancel or modify this tender at any time. A signed declaration of non-conflict-of-interest is mandatory. Applications must comply with all regional procurement guidelines. The grant is non-transferable and must be used within the stated jurisdiction.
