AU Innovation Hub — Agritech Development Grant 25

Sectoragritech
BudgetUSD 50,000
Deadline2026-08-13
RegionWest Africa

This grant supports innovative agritech solutions that address key development challenges across West Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.
