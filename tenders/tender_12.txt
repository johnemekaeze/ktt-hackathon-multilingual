TITLE: GreenAfrica Initiative — Wastetech Development Grant 12
ISSUING BODY: GreenAfrica Initiative
SECTOR: wastetech
BUDGET: USD 1,000,000
DEADLINE: 2026-09-07
REGION: West Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Cooperatives with a minimum of 20 registered members.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative wastetech solutions that address key development challenges across West Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
The issuing body reserves the right to cancel or modify this tender at any time. All submitted documents become the property of the issuing organization. Incomplete applications will be disqualified without further notice. Priority will be given to women-led and youth-led enterprises.
