TITLE: ECOWAS Development Bank — Healthtech Development Grant 14
ISSUING BODY: ECOWAS Development Bank
SECTOR: healthtech
BUDGET: USD 50,000
DEADLINE: 2026-11-05
REGION: Southern Africa
ELIGIBILITY: SMEs registered in AU member states with at least 2 years of operation.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative healthtech solutions that address key development challenges across Southern Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
Incomplete applications will be disqualified without further notice. Applications must comply with all regional procurement guidelines. Financial statements for the last three fiscal years must be attached. Priority will be given to women-led and youth-led enterprises.
