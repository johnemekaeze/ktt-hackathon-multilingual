TITLE: AU Innovation Hub — Agritech Development Grant 13
ISSUING BODY: AU Innovation Hub
SECTOR: agritech
BUDGET: USD 5,000
DEADLINE: 2027-01-14
REGION: Central Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Technology startups with a validated prototype or MVP.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative agritech solutions that address key development challenges across Central Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
Organizations with prior convictions for fraud are ineligible. Environmental and social impact assessments must accompany applications. Incomplete applications will be disqualified without further notice. Financial statements for the last three fiscal years must be attached.
