TITLE: AU Innovation Hub — Fintech Development Grant 23
ISSUING BODY: AU Innovation Hub
SECTOR: fintech
BUDGET: USD 200,000
DEADLINE: 2026-10-04
REGION: Central Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Community-based organizations with local government endorsement.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative fintech solutions that address key development challenges across Central Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
All submitted documents become the property of the issuing organization. Environmental and social impact assessments must accompany applications. The issuing body reserves the right to cancel or modify this tender at any time. A signed declaration of non-conflict-of-interest is mandatory.
