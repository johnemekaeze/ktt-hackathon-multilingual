ECOWAS Development Bank — Healthtech Development Grant 26

Sectorhealthtech
BudgetUSD 200,000
Deadline2026-06-01
RegionCentral Africa

This grant supports innovative healthtech solutions that address key development challenges across Central Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.
