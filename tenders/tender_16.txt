TITLE: SADC Grant Facility — Edtech Development Grant 16
ISSUING BODY: SADC Grant Facility
SECTOR: edtech
BUDGET: USD 1,000,000
DEADLINE: 2027-02-02
REGION: East Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Technology startups with a validated prototype or MVP.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative edtech solutions that address key development challenges across East Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
The grant is non-transferable and must be used within the stated jurisdiction. All submitted documents become the property of the issuing organization. Organizations with prior convictions for fraud are ineligible. The issuing body reserves the right to cancel or modify this tender at any time.
