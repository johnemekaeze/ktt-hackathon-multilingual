TITLE: USAID East Africa — Wastetech Development Grant 18
ISSUING BODY: USAID East Africa
SECTOR: wastetech
BUDGET: USD 50,000
DEADLINE: 2026-12-13
REGION: Central Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Cooperatives with a minimum of 20 registered members.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative wastetech solutions that address key development challenges across Central Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
Incomplete applications will be disqualified without further notice. All submitted documents become the property of the issuing organization. The grant is non-transferable and must be used within the stated jurisdiction. Applications must comply with all regional procurement guidelines.
