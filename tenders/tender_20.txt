TITLE: UNDP Africa — Healthtech Development Grant 20
ISSUING BODY: UNDP Africa
SECTOR: healthtech
BUDGET: USD 1,000,000
DEADLINE: 2026-06-24
REGION: Pan-Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Technology startups with a validated prototype or MVP.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative healthtech solutions that address key development challenges across Pan-Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
A signed declaration of non-conflict-of-interest is mandatory. The grant is non-transferable and must be used within the stated jurisdiction. Organizations with prior convictions for fraud are ineligible. Environmental and social impact assessments must accompany applications.
