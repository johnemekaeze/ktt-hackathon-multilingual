TITLE: SADC Grant Facility — Wastetech Development Grant 06
ISSUING BODY: SADC Grant Facility
SECTOR: wastetech
BUDGET: USD 50,000
DEADLINE: 2026-08-10
REGION: East Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Non-governmental organizations with proven track record in the stated sector.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative wastetech solutions that address key development challenges across East Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
Financial statements for the last three fiscal years must be attached. The issuing body reserves the right to cancel or modify this tender at any time. Environmental and social impact assessments must accompany applications. Incomplete applications will be disqualified without further notice.
