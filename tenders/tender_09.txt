TITLE: GIZ Africa Programme — Cleantech Development Grant 09
ISSUING BODY: GIZ Africa Programme
SECTOR: cleantech
BUDGET: USD 5,000
DEADLINE: 2026-10-20
REGION: Southern Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Community-based organizations with local government endorsement.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative cleantech solutions that address key development challenges across Southern Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
Financial statements for the last three fiscal years must be attached. Incomplete applications will be disqualified without further notice. The issuing body reserves the right to cancel or modify this tender at any time. Applications must comply with all regional procurement guidelines.
