EAC Secretariat — Cleantech Development Grant 27

Sectorcleantech
BudgetUSD 1,000,000
Deadline2026-09-23
RegionSouthern Africa

This grant supports innovative cleantech solutions that address key development challenges across Southern Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.
