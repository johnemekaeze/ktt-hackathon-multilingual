TITLE: World Bank Africa Office — Agritech Development Grant 07
ISSUING BODY: World Bank Africa Office
SECTOR: agritech
BUDGET: USD 200,000
DEADLINE: 2026-07-11
REGION: West Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Cooperatives with a minimum of 20 registered members.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative agritech solutions that address key development challenges across West Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
Financial statements for the last three fiscal years must be attached. Organizations with prior convictions for fraud are ineligible. Applications must comply with all regional procurement guidelines. Priority will be given to women-led and youth-led enterprises.
