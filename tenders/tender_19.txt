TITLE: GIZ Africa Programme — Agritech Development Grant 19
ISSUING BODY: GIZ Africa Programme
SECTOR: agritech
BUDGET: USD 200,000
DEADLINE: 2026-06-16
REGION: Southern Africa
ELIGIBILITY: SMEs registered in AU member states with at least 2 years of operation.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative agritech solutions that address key development challenges across Southern Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
All submitted documents become the property of the issuing organization. Priority will be given to women-led and youth-led enterprises. A signed declaration of non-conflict-of-interest is mandatory. Organizations with prior convictions for fraud are ineligible.
