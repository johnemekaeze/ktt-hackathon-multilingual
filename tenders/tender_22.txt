TITLE: GreenAfrica Initiative — Edtech Development Grant 22
ISSUING BODY: GreenAfrica Initiative
SECTOR: edtech
BUDGET: USD 50,000
DEADLINE: 2026-10-20
REGION: West Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Technology startups with a validated prototype or MVP.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative edtech solutions that address key development challenges across West Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
All submitted documents become the property of the issuing organization. The grant is non-transferable and must be used within the stated jurisdiction. Applications must comply with all regional procurement guidelines. Financial statements for the last three fiscal years must be attached.
