TITLE: ECOWAS Development Bank — Edtech Development Grant 04
ISSUING BODY: ECOWAS Development Bank
SECTOR: edtech
BUDGET: USD 1,000,000
DEADLINE: 2026-09-01
REGION: Southern Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Community-based organizations with local government endorsement.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative edtech solutions that address key development challenges across Southern Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
A signed declaration of non-conflict-of-interest is mandatory. Incomplete applications will be disqualified without further notice. The grant is non-transferable and must be used within the stated jurisdiction. Organizations with prior convictions for fraud are ineligible.
