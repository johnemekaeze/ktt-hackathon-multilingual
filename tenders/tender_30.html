USAID East Africa — Wastetech Development Grant 30

Sectorwastetech
BudgetUSD 200,000
Deadline2026-09-02
RegionWest Africa

This grant supports innovative wastetech solutions that address key development challenges across West Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.
