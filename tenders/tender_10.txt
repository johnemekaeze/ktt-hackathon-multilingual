TITLE: UNDP Africa — Edtech Development Grant 10
ISSUING BODY: UNDP Africa
SECTOR: edtech
BUDGET: USD 50,000
DEADLINE: 2026-09-16
REGION: Pan-Africa
ELIGIBILITY: Cooperatives with a minimum of 20 registered members.

DESCRIPTION:
This grant supports innovative edtech solutions that address key development challenges across Pan-Africa. Funded projects must demonstrate measurable impact on at least 500 beneficiaries within the first year of implementation. Applicants are expected to present a detailed work plan, budget breakdown, and monitoring framework aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals.

ADMINISTRATIVE NOTES:
The issuing body reserves the right to cancel or modify this tender at any time. Incomplete applications will be disqualified without further notice. Priority will be given to women-led and youth-led enterprises. Environmental and social impact assessments must accompany applications.
